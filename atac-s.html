❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

❓ Ye symbols kahan use kar sakte hain? ▼

Yeh symbols har jagah use ho sakte hain! Instagram bio, WhatsApp status, Facebook posts, TikTok comments, YouTube channel names, gaming profiles, Twitter bios, LinkedIn headlines, Pinterest pins, Snapchat names, aur bhi bahut kuch. Jo bhi jagah text likh sakte ho, wahan ye symbols attach ho jayenge. Kuch apps mein font thoda different dikh sakta hai, par mostly sab jagah kaam karte hain.

❓ Ye symbols mobile par bhi kaam karte hain? ▼

Bilkul! Mobile phones par bhi ye symbols perfectly kaam karte hain. Android ho ya iPhone, dono mein copy-paste kar sakte ho. WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook mobile apps mein bhi ye sahi se dikhenge. Bas symbol copy karo aur apni app mein paste kardo - automatically attach ho jayega!

❓ Har symbol attach kyun hota hai? ▼

Kyunki ye sab "combining characters" hain. Unicode mein kuch special characters hote hain jo previous character ke saath merge ho jate hain. Isliye jab aap A ke baad ͙ lagate ho to A͙ banta hai. Humne website mein sirf aise hi symbols include kiye hain jo attach hote hain. Har symbol ke saath "✅ Attach" badge hai iska proof.

❓ Repeat count ka maximum kitna hai? ▼

Humne 1x se 10x tak ka option rakha hai. Aap chahe to 10x tak repeat kar sakte hain, matlab ek letter ke baad 10 symbols lagenge. Jaise A̶̶̶̶̶̶̶̶̶̶ (10 baar cut line). Isse bahut unique styles bana sakte ho. Agar aur chahiye to hum code mein badha sakte hain!

❓ Kya ye symbols free hain? ▼

Haan, 100% free! Koi payment nahi, koi login nahi. Bas website kholo, symbol choose karo, copy karo aur use karo. Humne 35+ symbols diye hain sab free mein. Chahe to 1000+ combinations bana sakte ho.

❓ Mera favorite symbol nahi mila to? ▼

Agar aapko koi aur symbol chahiye jo yahan nahi hai, to humein batao! Main code mein add kar dunga. Jaise aapne ᪲᪲᪲ style mangaya tha, waise hi aur bhi symbols add kar sakte hain. Bas batane ki der hai!
