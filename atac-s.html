❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I use these symbols? ▼

Everywhere! Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, gaming profiles, Twitter, bios, comments, and more. Anywhere you can type text, these symbols will attach. They work perfectly on all social media platforms and messaging apps.

Do they work on mobile? ▼

Absolutely! Both Android and iOS support these combining characters. Just copy and paste into any app. They display perfectly on all modern devices.

Why does every symbol attach? ▼

Because these are Unicode "combining characters" designed specifically to merge with the previous character. Each symbol card has a "✅ Attaches" badge to confirm it will attach properly to any letter or emoji you put before it.

Can I add my own suggestions? ▼

Yes! Click the "Add Your Own" button above and share your favorite symbol combinations with the community. Your suggestions will be visible to everyone and saved in your browser.

How many symbols can I attach? ▼

You can attach up to 10 symbols per character using the repeat buttons (1x to 10x). This creates unique layered effects. Feel free to experiment!
